Copyright / DMCA Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

FrameFlow respects third-party copyright and removes infringing content in response to valid claims. If you believe your work is being used in the FrameFlow catalog without permission, send a notice following the procedure below.

1. Designated agent

Contact for copyright claims: dmca@getframeflow.app. You can also fill out the form below — it goes directly to the moderation team.

2. What a takedown notice must include

  1. Identification of the infringed work (what is protected).
  2. The location of the infringing material on FrameFlow (template name, URL, or ID).
  3. Your contact details (name, email).
  4. A statement of good-faith belief that the use of the material is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.
  5. Confirmation that the information in the notice is accurate and that you are the rights holder (or their authorized representative).

3. What we do

Upon a valid notice, we remove the template in question from the catalog/downloads (takedown) and notify the uploader. Repeat infringers' accounts may be terminated.

4. Counter-notice

If you believe your content was removed in error, you may send a counter-notice via dmca@getframeflow.app — include the material and a good-faith statement supporting its reinstatement.

5. Report an infringement

Fill out the form below. False claims may lead to liability.
