# Brend nomi — research va tavsiyalar

> Maqsad: "AssetFlow" ni qayta nomlash. Kreativ assets + AI platforma, global auditoriya.
> Manba: web research (raqobat manzarasi, naming best-practice, trademark/domain). 2026-06.
> ⚠️ Domen/trademark JONLI tekshirilmagan — qaror oldidan §oxiridagi checklist bilan tasdiqlash shart.

## Asosiy topilma
- **"AssetFlow" allaqachon band** — bir nechta faol kompaniya, jumladan **Cannes mukofotini olgan film/TV asset platformasi** (assetflow.co.uk) + inventar SaaS. Qayta nomlash to'g'ri qaror.
- **Tasviriy nomlar zaif:** trademark qilish qiyin, .com odatda band, ajralib turmaydi.
- **Suggestive/coined (ishora qiluvchi) nomlar kuchli:** ownable, .com imkoniyati yaxshiroq, brendlanadi.
- **Muhim dalil:** aynan shu nишада (stock + AI) bozor lideri **Freepik → Magnific** ga o'tdi (2026-apr) — coined nom tanladi, tasviriy emas.

## Band/qochish kerak

## TOP-3 tavsiya
1. **Framory** — eng muvozanatli: coined-meaningful (frame=video, -ory=joy), suggestive (eng kuchli TM turi), qisqa, global aniq, .com imkoniyati yuqori.
2. **Renderly** — eng "kreativ-tech" hissi: render = AE/AI yadrosi + zamonaviy -ly.
3. **Studiate** — "Studio" equity'ni saqlaydi (mahsulot allaqachon Studio+plugin), coined, ownable.

## Tekshirish checklist (qaror oldidan har finalist uchun)
**Domen:** .com avval (Namecheap/GoDaddy) + .io/.co/.app/.ai; WHOIS (parked vs faol biznes); ijtimoiy handle'lar.
**Trademark (global = 3 yurisdiksiya):**
- USPTO (AQSh): yangi Trademark Search (uspto.gov/trademarks/search) — phonetik+variant, Class 9 (software), 42 (SaaS), 41 (media)
- EUIPO (EU): eSearch plus / TMview
- WIPO: Global Brand Database + Madrid Monitor
- Qo'shni sohalarni ham (film/media — Cannes AssetFlow kabi)
- G'olib uchun trademark advokat clearance
