POPULAR ANSWERS

Frequently asked

How do credits work?−

Every AI generation shows its signed cost first. Monthly plan credits reset each billing cycle; purchased credit packs stay until used.

Why was my generation refunded?+

Eligible provider failures and blocked references restore the charged credits automatically.

Why can’t I import a template?+

The pack may still be preview-only, your Free download limit may be reached, or the plugin account may need a fresh Sync.

How do contributor payouts work?+

Earnings accrue from unique subscriber downloads and are tracked in Contributor Studio under Earnings & payouts.
