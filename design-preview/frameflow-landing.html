FAQ

Questions Answered

What is FrameFlow? −
FrameFlow is an all-in-one creative platform offering unlimited access to professional video templates, AI generation tools (image, video, voice, SFX), stock footage, sound effects, and graphic assets — all accessible inside After Effects and Premiere Pro via a native plugin.
Can I use assets in commercial projects? +
Yes. All FrameFlow templates, stock footage, sound effects, and AI-generated content are cleared for commercial use with an active subscription. Your completed projects are protected under our commercial license for the duration of your subscription.
How does the After Effects plugin work? +
The FrameFlow plugin installs as a CEP panel inside After Effects (and Premiere Pro). Browse the entire library, search with AI-powered semantic search, run AI generation, and import any asset directly into your active composition — no switching apps required.
What are AI Credits? +
AI Credits are used for generative features: image generation, video generation, AI voiceover, and custom SFX generation. Credits reset monthly. Unused credits do not roll over. You can see a cost preview before each generation — there are no surprise charges.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +
Yes, you can cancel anytime. Once your billing cycle ends, your account reverts to free access. Projects you've completed during your paid subscription remain covered under the license. No long-term contracts or commitments.
Is there a free plan? +
Yes. Free accounts can browse the catalog and download free-tier templates and assets with watermarks. To access the full library, AI generation, stock video, and SFX, upgrade to Starter, Pro, or Max.
